Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Overview

Spendly ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have over it. By using Spendly, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter manually
  • Usage data — basic logs such as login timestamps, used to detect suspicious activity

We do not collect payment information, government identifiers, or data from third-party financial institutions.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate and improve Spendly:

  • To display your expense history and summaries
  • To authenticate your account securely
  • To diagnose bugs and improve performance

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any automated decision-making.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. We may disclose data only if required by law or to protect the security of the App and its users.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secured servers. We use industry-standard measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and hashed passwords to protect your information. No method of storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account and expense data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export your expense data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

8. Cookies

Spendly uses a single session cookie to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or any third-party cookies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will indicate the date of the most recent revision at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us at privacy@spendly.app.
